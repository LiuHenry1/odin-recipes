College Ramen

Description

The perfect choice for a college student who can barely afford rent. Sodium may be too high but that's not an issue because they're still young.

Ingredients

  • Any packet of ramen
  • One egg
  • Black garlic oil

Steps

  1. Prepare a pot of boiling water.
  2. Add noodles to the pot.
  3. Prepare a bowl with black garlic oil on the bottom.
  4. Once noodles are cooked, pour it in the bowl.
  5. Add seasoning packet.
  6. Boil egg and add it to the bowl.
