Fried Rice

Description

A popular dish in Chinese cuisine. A combination of rice, eggs, vegetables, meat, and aromatics that ends up creating a simple yet delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Peas
  • Choice of meat
  • Two eggs
  • Cup of leftover rice
  • Soysauce

Steps

  1. Heat up the wok.
  2. Dice the onions, garlic, carrot and peas.
  3. Break two eggs into a small bowl and whisk.
  4. Season choice of meat and dice.
  5. When the wok is heated, cook the meat first.
  6. Once cooked, take them out and add aromatics and vegetables
  7. Add the eggs, once they solidify a bit, add the rice.
  8. Add the meat back in and pour soysauce around the rim.
