Toast

Description

A simple way to have delicious bread. Gone are the days of having plain bread. Easy way to add flavor, texture, and crunch.

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Butter
  • Topping of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet.
  2. Once heated, put in a stick of butter.
  3. Press bread and cook for a minute or so until golden brown.
  4. Flip bread and repeat last step.
  5. Take out and add topping of choice.
